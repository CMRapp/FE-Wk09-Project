Welcome Florida Beer Lovers!

If you love craft beers and you love Florida - you're in the right place!

Beer Buddy Florida initially started as a project while learning back end software development during Promineo Tech's Back End Software Developer Bootcamp. It is now on its way to becoming a FULL STACK development project as I am currently enrolled in the Front End Software Developer Bootcamp.

The purpose of this website is to provide information of Florida Craft Breweries and their beers. Information that you'll find (eventtually!) will include:

  • Brewery information, such as Date Established, Location, and Contact Information.
  • A list of brewer's beers including Pictures, Brewer's Notes, ABV & IBU.
  • Taster's Reviews (future)
  • Information on the Styles (Categories) of beer.

Pop a top of your favorite brew and dive on in!

NOTE: This website has been created solely using HTML and Bootstrap. No extra CSS, SASS or Javascript has been used to create the look you see.

The number of craft breweries have been rapidly growing in the United States over the last decade. According to the Brewers Association, in 2022 the number of microbreweries, brewpubs, taproom breweries and regional craft breweries reached an all-time high of 9,552! Source: Brewers Association

Florida only had 3 Craft breweries in 1989. The surge of Florida Craft Breweries began in the early 90s and has been growing every day since. As of 2022, there were almost 400 operational microbreweries in Florida. Here are the number of breweries by county.
